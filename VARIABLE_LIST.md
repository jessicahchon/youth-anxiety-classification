## D. Excluded Variables (Mentioned in Methods for Leakage Prevention)

| Variable Code | Variable Label | Reason for Exclusion |
|---------------|----------------|----------------------|
| **K2Q32A** | Depression diagnosis | High comorbidity with anxiety (68%); bidirectional relationship |
| **K2Q33B** | Anxiety—currently has condition | Direct leakage (part of diagnosis pathway) |
| **K2Q33C** | Anxiety severity | Direct leakage (severity rating) |
| **K4Q22_R** | Mental health treatment | Direct leakage (treatment utilization) |
| **K4Q23** | Medication for emotions/behavior | Direct leakage (treatment-related) |
| **K4Q27** | Needed healthcare not received | Direct leakage (unmet need) |
| **K4Q28X04** | Needed mental health services not received | Direct leakage (mental health-specific unmet need) |
| **SC_CSHCN** | Children with special healthcare needs screener | Composite variable derived from multiple health conditions |
| **HCABILITY** | Health affects ability | Direct functional consequence of health conditions |
| **SC_K2Q10–K2Q18** | Prescription medication & functional limitation variables | Part of CSHCN screener; treatment-related |
| **MEMORYCOND** | Serious difficulty concentrating/remembering/deciding | Overlaps with anxiety/depression symptoms |
| **ARRANGEHC** | Hours spent arranging healthcare | Treatment burden proxy; leakage risk |
